\n \n Septr. 6th Thursday 1804\n

\n

\na Storm this morning from the N. W. which lasted a fiew minits, we Set out and proceeded on \u00a0\u00a0 passed the head of the Isd. which is Seperated from the L. S \u00a0\u00a0 by a narrow Channel, a hard wind from the N. W. a verry Cold day\u2014 \u00a0\u00a0 we Camped on the S. S. at the upper point of Some timber, Some time before night, no timber, no timber being in reach.

\n

\nI saw Several goats on the hills on the S. S. also Buffalow in great numbers\u2014\n

[Ordway]

\n \n
\u00a0
\n \n

\n Thursday 6th \u00a0\u00a0 a cloudy morning \u00a0\u00a0 the wind from N. W. We Set off eairly, got fast Several times on the Sand bars. \u00a0\u00a0 could not make Much headway with the oars nor poles & were oblidged to cross over on N. S. & make use of the Towing line.\u2014 \u00a0\u00a0 the current Swift & Shallow. \u00a0\u00a0 we came to verry long Strait high Raged yallow L S. clifts S. S. Colter Came to the Boat \u00a0\u00a0 N. B. the Boat got fast on the Sand bar the L S. Swung the men all out \u00a0\u00a0 Got [whiskey?] \u00a0\u00a0 had not found Shannon nor the horses But had killed one Buffelow, one Elk 3 Deer one wolf 5 Turkies & one Goose\u2014 \u00a0\u00a0 one Beaver also \u00a0\u00a0 rainy & cold weather.\u2014 \u00a0\u00a0 Reuben Fields killed 1 Deer & 1 faun.\u2014 \u00a0\u00a0 we Camped at a handsome Bottom prarie on N. S. above a fine Grove of C. wood Timber.\u2014\n\n\n

\n \n
\n
\n \n

[Gass]

\n \n
\u00a0
\n \n

\n Thursday 6th. \u00a0\u00a0 We set out early and had cloudy morning: passed a handsome bottom prairie on the north side; at the upper end of which is a grove of cotton wood, and a long range of dark coloured bluffs on the south side. About 9 o'clock it began to rain and we had strong wind ahead. There are a great number of sand bars, and we had much difficulty in getting along. We encamped on the north side and one of our men [3] killed two deer.\n\n

\n \n
\n
\n \n

[Whitehouse]

\n \n
\u00a0
\n \n

\n Thursday 6th Sept. \u00a0\u00a0 a cloudy morning.\u2014 \u00a0\u00a0 Several hunters out hunting. \u00a0\u00a0 colter joined us \u00a0\u00a0 had not found Shannon. \u00a0\u00a0 the hunters killed 1 Buffalow one Elk 3 Deer one woolf 1 Deer & four Turkies. \u00a0\u00a0 Camped on N. S.

\n

\nThursday Septemr 6th \u00a0\u00a0 We started early this morning, it being cloudy weather; We sent out our hunters, in the Evening they returned; but had not found Shannon, the Man who was Missing; they brought in with them, One \nBuffalo, One Elk, three deer, One Wolf & four Turkies, we encamped on the North side of the River.\u2014\n\n
